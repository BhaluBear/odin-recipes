Spicy Parmesean Chicken

Spicy and Tasty Low Carb, Low Calorie Parmesean Crusted Chicken

This spicty parmesean crusted chicken is made for anyone wanting a diversion from their ordinary chicken dinner that kicks the flavor up a notch without sacrificing your waistline. Low-calorie mayonnaise keeps the chicken moist during baking and the mix of spices will keep you coming back for more. Mix in your favorite parmesan reggiano and you can't help but have a good time.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 (8-ounce) chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 425 degrees F
  2. Combine all of the ingredients (except the chicekn) into a bowl, mixing well
  3. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil, lightly oil to prevent sticking
  4. Place chicken on baking sheet, then cover evenly with ingredient mixture
  5. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, until internal chicken temperature registers 165 degrees F
  6. Enjoy!
