Roasted Sweet Potatoes

Seasoned and Perfectly Roasted Sweet Potatoes

These sweet potatoes are delicious on their own or paired with another protein. They are perfectly flavored with 6 different commonly owned seasonings. Cooked to perfection, the outside is lightly crunchy while the inside is left soft and delicate. Don't wait to try this side dish out tonight!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds (3 large) sweet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3/4 teaspoon fine sea salt (kosher salt)
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly cracked pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 425 degrees F
  2. Peel and cube sweet potatoes into 1/2 inch sized pieces
  3. Toss sweet potatoes on large sheet pan with olive oil
  4. Combine all seasonings in a small bowl before topping over sweet potatoes in sheet pan
  5. Roast in oven for a total of 27-35 minutes, flipping after 10-15 minutes as needed
  6. Enjoy!
